# Project Summary & Business Context

This document outlines the business objectives, target audience, user personas, and product requirements for the Anshay Basene Portfolio project.

---

## 1. Business Context & Vision

- **Context**: In the saturated landscape of video editors and designers, stand-out visual presentation is a primary trust indicator. This portfolio serves as a showcase of premium editing.
- **Vision**: Create a responsive website that mimics a cinematic editing bay, incorporating timelines, timecode counts, tactile cards, and synced before/after progression grids to highlight the post-production value.
- **Goals**:
  - Convert cold traffic into warm inquiries via an estimation calculator.
  - Simplify content updates so the editor can manage projects and copy from the browser.
  - Ensure zero scroll lag across low-power mobile devices.

---

## 2. Target Audience & User Personas

### 1. YouTube Content Creators
- **Need**: Highly engaging, paced storytelling edits to drive watch time and retention.
- **Pain Point**: Finding editors who understand pacing, sound effects, and grading depth.

### 2. Marketing Agency Directors
- **Need**: Fast turnaround, reliability, and structured project revisions.
- **Pain Point**: Lack of clear pricing parameters and difficult asset pipelines.

### 3. Real Estate Developers
- **Need**: Elegant cinematic property walkthroughs with high production value.
- **Pain Point**: Editors who cannot coordinate lighting, color grading, and timing.

---

## 3. Product Requirements Document (PRD)

| Feature | Requirement | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Passcode Validation** | Authenticate admin panel requests safely on the server. | High |
| **Calculator Estimator** | Estimate project rates dynamically based on seconds and edits complexity. | High |
| **Synced Video Progressions** | Sync raw, rough cut, color/sfx, and final clips in a 4-player grid using the YouTube API. | High |
| **Adaptive Motion System** | Auto-detect device performance thresholds and throttle animations accordingly. | High |
| **Media Uploads** | Allow PNG, JPG, JPEG, and WEBP image uploads under 5MB. | Medium |
